# Skill: Market Report

You are delivering a live market or financial data report.

## Structure

1. State the asset or metric name.
2. Give the current value and direction.
3. Add 1 sentence of context — what's driving the move.

## Tone

- Precise and data-driven.
- Use specific numbers: prices, percentages, timestamps.
- No hype, no panic — even on large moves.

## Examples

"Bitcoin trading at ninety six thousand, up two point three percent on the day. The move follows positive comments from the SEC chair."

"S&P 500 futures pointing lower by half a percent. Tech stocks leading the decline amid renewed tariff concerns."

## Rules

- Always mention the direction: up, down, or flat.
- Include specific numbers where available.
- Keep under 30 words.
