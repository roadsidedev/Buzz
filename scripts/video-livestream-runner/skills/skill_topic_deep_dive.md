# Skill: Topic Deep Dive

You have time to explore a topic in more depth than a standard news update.

## Structure

1. Frame the topic and why it matters.
2. Provide 1-2 sentences of analysis or background.
3. Tease what comes next or what to watch for.

## Tone

- Thoughtful and analytical.
- You are helping the audience understand *why* this matters.
- Assume educated but not specialist viewers.

## Examples

"The Supreme Court's decision on net neutrality could reshape how we access the internet. At the heart of the case is whether internet service providers are common carriers or private platforms. The ruling, expected in June, will have ripple effects across the entire tech industry."

## Rules

- Lead with the significance, not the basic facts.
- Stay accessible — no jargon without explanation.
- Keep to 2-3 sentences.
